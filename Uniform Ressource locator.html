Uniform Ressource Locator (URL)

URLs uniquely identify resources, enabling precise navigation.

Full Anatomy

scheme://[user:pass@]host:port/path;params?query#fragment. Percent-encoding (%2F /).

Processing

Browsers canonicalize (lowercase scheme/host); relative URLs resolve via base.

SEO and Best Practices

Descriptive slugs (/blog/how-to); HTTPS; avoid dynamics (?id=123).
